Stefan Humm
Software Engineer

I was born and raised up in a city called Laupheim in Germany. After school I did an apprenticeship at Robert Bosch GmbH and worked there before I went to University of Media in Stuttgart to study Media Informatics. During University I went to Tokyo to do an internship half a year. After graduation from university I decided to go again to Japan. So currently I'm living in Tokyo working as software engineer and enjoy the beauty of Japan and the culture.

Software Developing fascinates me since I was a child. To know that thousands of people use my software making their daily life easier motivates me to create high quality software. Next to Software Developing my passion are sports and photography which have no limits of creativity.
